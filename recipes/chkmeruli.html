Chkmeruli

Description

Chkmeruli is a Georgian dish made of chicken, fried and then cooked in a garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 head garlic, chopped
  • 1 thumb fresh ginger
  • 1 cup milk (more or less)
  • 1 cup heavy cream (more or less)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp red ajika paste
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into 8 pieces, plus wings, and season it with salt, pepper and coriander
  2. Crown the chicken in a cast iron skillet
  3. Add a splash of wine to the pan and put it in the oven
  4. One head of garlic and ginger in a garlic press
  5. Put them into anoter pan along with milk and cream, enough to cover the chicken
  6. Add ajika
  7. Five minutes before the chicken is ready, pour the sauce on it
  8. Enjoy
